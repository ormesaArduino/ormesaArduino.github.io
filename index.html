Welcome!

Welcome to this website! Here you will find information about how to use Arduino and Arduino programming. With an Arduino, you can control a variety of devices such as LCD screens, LEDs lights, motors, buzzers, and much more. You can control these devices with buttons, switches, dials, and other sensors. The intent of these tutorials is to teach you the fundamentals of electronic circuits and how to use them with an Arduino. This includes both programming in Arduino code (C++) and how to physically wire some of the devices mentioned above to your Arduino. Hopefully, after going through these lessons you will have a basic understanding of Arduino, its limitations, and a springboard for designing your MESA project. Have fun!
